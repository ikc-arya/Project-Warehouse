  • Shipping information

    1. Delivery of the ordered product will be prepared for delivery as soon as payment is confirmed.

    2. Same-day delivery is only available for deposits made by 2pm (14:00).

    On the delivery date, the status is 'Preparing for delivery', and if you enter the invoice number from the courier on the afternoon of the day of delivery, the status will be changed to 'Delivery'.

    Delivery tracking can be checked at the courier company the day after the delivery date.

    We ask for your understanding as it is difficult for us to confirm the delivery date because the delivery schedule of a courier company may vary by sales office.

  • Packaging information

    1. When using courier for general goods, box packaging is the default, and to protect the goods, they are packed without shaking with sponge, styrofoam, air cap (air vinyl, bubble wrap), etc.
